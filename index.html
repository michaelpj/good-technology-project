Solving important problems through technology entrepreneurship

We're a research project and consultancy focussed on social impact in technology and entrepreneurship. We offer:

  • Help finding important problems to work on
  • Impact strategy and measurement
  • Evaluation of ideas, companies, and founders
  • Connections to experts and other resources
  • Career strategy

We're particularly interested in working with entrepreneurs who are looking for important problems to work on, and entrepreneurship institutions, such as accelerators, who are aiming to have as much impact as possible.
